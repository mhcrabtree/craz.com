Our Story

Where Curiosity Meets Chaos (In the Best Way Possible)

Crabtree Labz started as a family sketchbook; a place where half-finished doodles, wild ideas, and late-night experiments coexisted. Over time, those small sparks grew into mascots, worlds, and an entire creative ecosystem built on color, humor, and heart. We set out to build a playground: a place where imagination is the whole point.

A Family-Owned Creative Studio

Everything we create begins at home:

  • A conversation at the table
  • A joke that spirals out of control
  • A character someone sketched on a napkin

Crabtree Labz thrives on collaboration, real collaboration, the kind that comes from people who understand each other’s quirks, strengths, and creative chaos. Every mascot, every brand, every experiment carries a bit of that shared energy.

Experiments Become Worlds

We believe creativity is most effective when it resembles play. So we treat every idea like an experiment:

  • Imagining a unicorn as a rogue trickster
  • Imagining a dog mascot running a tech company
  • Imagining a lab flask containing every color of the rainbow
  • Imagining a full studio built around curiosity itself

Some experiments turn into full brands.
Some become stories.
Some just make us laugh.

Why We Exist

Crabtree Labz is here to create things that feel alive:

  • Mascots with personality
  • Worlds with heart
  • Brands with a sense of wonder

We are not chasing perfection.
We are chasing joy.

What Comes Next

Crabtree Labz is a creative playground. This is just the beginning. New characters, new brands, and new experiments will grow from the same spark that started it all.
