Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Crabtree Labz respects your privacy. This website (craz.com) is intentionally simple and does not collect personal data unless you choose to share it with us.

This policy explains what we do and what we do not do.

1. No Cookies

This site does not use any of the following:

  • Cookies
  • Tracking pixels
  • Local storage
  • Session storage
  • Analytics scripts
  • Third party embeds that set cookies

Your browser is not tracked, profiled, or monitored in any way.

2. Minimal JavaScript and No Tracking

This site uses a small amount of JavaScript only to power the contact form. The script does not track you or analyze your behavior.

We do not use JavaScript for any of the following:

  • Analytics
  • Fingerprinting
  • Behavioral tracking
  • Advertising
  • Social media integrations
  • Hidden scripts
  • Data collection tools

The contact form script runs in your browser and sends only the information you choose to submit. No other JavaScript is used anywhere on the site.

3. Information You Choose to Send

The only information we receive is the information you voluntarily provide. This includes messages you send through the contact form or by email.

If you choose to share information, we do the following:

  • We use it only to respond to you
  • We do not sell it
  • We do not share it
  • We do not use it for marketing
  • We do not store it longer than necessary

4. Server Logs

Our hosting provider may generate basic server logs as part of normal web hosting operations. These logs may include your IP address, browser type, and timestamp.

Crabtree Labz handles server logs in the following ways:

  • We do not access logs unless needed for security or troubleshooting
  • We do not combine logs with any other data
  • We do not use logs for analytics or tracking

5. Third Party Services

This site does not use any of the following:

  • Google Analytics
  • Social media plugins
  • Advertising networks
  • Embedded third party content

6. Children’s Privacy

Crabtree Labz does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy if we introduce new features that involve data collection, cookies, or analytics.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the Contact page.
